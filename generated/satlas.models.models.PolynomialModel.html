satlas.models.models.PolynomialModel¶

class satlas.models.models.PolynomialModel(args)[source]¶

Constructs a polynomial response.

__init__(args)[source]¶

PolynomialModel creates a general polynomial of the order given by len(args)-1. The given coefficients are ordered lowest to highest order.

Parameters:args (iterable of values) – Iterable containing all the values for the coefficients. Polynomial order is determined by the length. args[0] is the coefficient of order 0, etc...
